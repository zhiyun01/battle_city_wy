BATTLE CITY

LEVEL 1
SCORE 0
LIVES 3

How To Play

Drive your tank, protect the base, and clear the enemy tanks.

Controls

Move: Arrow keys or WASD

Shoot: Space

Sound: Start turns on arcade music and effects. Use ♪ to mute.

Goal

Destroy enemies before they destroy your base.

You lose a life if your tank gets hit. You lose the round if the base is destroyed.

Brick: blocks tanks and bullets, can be destroyed.
Steel: blocks tanks and bullets, very strong.
Water: tanks cannot drive through it, bullets fly over it.
Forest: tanks can hide under it.
Ice: open ground with a slippery look.
Mud: slows tanks down.
Lava: hurts the player tank.
Arrow road: speeds tanks up.
Speed boost: fast floor tile.
Bounce pad: open special floor tile.
Force field: blocks tanks and bullets.
Base: protect this. If it is hit, game over.
